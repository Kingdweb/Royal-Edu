How to Dance Wrestle

Follow these steps to learn the art of dance wrestling:

  1. Warm up with basic stretches to prevent injury.
  2. Learn basic wrestling holds and positions.
  3. Add rhythmic movements to your holds, syncing with music.
  4. Incorporate spins and footwork to create dynamic transitions.
  5. Practice your moves with a partner to improve coordination.
  6. Showcase your routine by creating an engaging performance.

Additional Tips:

  • Always communicate with your partner to ensure safety.
  • Have fun and express your personality through your movements.
  • Practice regularly to enhance your skills.
